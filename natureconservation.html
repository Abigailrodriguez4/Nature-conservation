Global Warming Awareness

Mankind has done many great things, but they aren't always for the best. Our world is getting warmer and warmer because of Climate Change and that is not a good thing.

What causes Climate Change?

Fossil Fuels such as coal, oil ,and Natural gases are the major contributors to Climate Change Causing the vast majority of Green House Gas Emissions.

What are some affects of Climate Change?

As the Earth's temperatures increase,extreme weather and sea levels rising becoming very big threats to homes all over the world.In the past few years the United States has seen major hurricanes, floods,droughts,wildfires,and they are all linked to climate change

How can we help?

Here are some small changes you can make to help stop climate change...

      Ride a bike or walk when the weather is nice instead of taking an automobile.
      Wash your hands with warm or cold water, not hot water.
      Eat meat free meals with home grown fruits and vegetables.(Eat organic)
      Don't waste food. You can start a compost bin.
      Buy local!
      Line dry your clothes.
      Offset you carbon emissions.
      Start a small garden, to grow your own fruits and vegetables.
      Unplug devices when you aren't using them.

    REDUCE! REUSE! RECYCLE!
